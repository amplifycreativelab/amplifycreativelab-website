# 3‑Month Social Media Content Plan (Cross‑post: Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest, YouTube)

**Assumptions**
- Brand: Amplify Creative Lab (Perth, AU) — food + product photography + hospitality marketing/web.
- Start any Monday; use the weekly cadence below and repeat for 12 weeks.
- Primary goal: drive enquiries/bookings + grow email list/retargeting audiences.

## Posting cadence (simple + sustainable)

**Instagram + Facebook (cross‑post)**
- **3 feed posts/week**: **Mon 6:00pm**, **Wed 12:00pm**, **Fri 6:00pm** (Perth time)
- Optional (if you have time): 2–3 Stories/week repurposed from feed posts (no extra planning required)

**Pinterest**
- **1–2 pins/day, 5–6 days/week** (mostly repurposed from IG/FB posts)
- For every IG/FB post, publish **3 Pinterest pins** (same URL, different headline/creative)

**YouTube (when possible)**
- **2 posts/month** (image‑led): slideshow “case study”, “before/after”, or “3 tips” with captions/voiceover

## Content pillars (rotate weekly)
1. **Portfolio proof** (results + before/after)
2. **Education** (photo + marketing + website tips)
3. **Process/BTS** (how you work, what to expect)
4. **Social proof** (testimonials, client wins, FAQs)
5. **Offers/CTAs** (lead magnet, consultation, seasonal push)

## Asset templates to prepare once (speeds everything up)
- 3 Canva templates for Pinterest pins (1000×1500): **“Before/After”**, **“3 Tips”**, **“Case Study”**
- 1 quote/testimonial template (square + vertical)
- A consistent CTA link destination (pick one): `/contact/`, a booking link, or a lead magnet landing page

## Weekly plan (12 weeks)

Each week includes:
- **Mon (Portfolio/Proof)**: one strong image (or carousel)
- **Wed (Education)**: one graphic + supporting image
- **Fri (Process/Social proof/Offer)**: testimonial/BTS/mini‑offer
- **Pinterest**: 3 pin variants per post (headlines included)
- **YouTube**: every other week (optional)

## Pinterest execution (quick rules)
- For every IG/FB post, create **3 pin variants**:
  - Variant A: bold outcome headline (“Menu photos that sell”)
  - Variant B: “how‑to” headline (“3 lighting fixes”)
  - Variant C: local keyword headline (“Perth food photographer”)
- Always include a **keyword title + 1–2 sentence description + link** (prefer a relevant page/blog post).
- Boards to create/use: “Perth Food Photography”, “Restaurant Marketing Tips”, “Product Photography”, “Website Tips for Hospitality”, “Branding & Content Strategy”.

## YouTube execution (image‑led, minimal edit)
- Keep it simple: **8–12 images** + on‑screen captions + light music (optional).
- Add a clear CTA in description: “Enquire: https://amplifycreativelab.com/contact/”.

## Quick checklist each week
- Pick 3 posts from the week; pull images from your latest shoots/portfolio.
- Batch‑write captions (20–30 minutes).
- Batch‑create Pinterest pins (30–60 minutes).
- Schedule everything (Meta + Pinterest scheduler).
